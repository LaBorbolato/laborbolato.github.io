I am a PhD student at the Universidade de São Paulo in Brasil. My research is focused on analyzing the structures of the Milky Way's disk, from its formation to the substructures of the outer disk produced by the disk's interaction with accreting dwarf galaxies. The chemistry and dynamics of disk stars can tell us about our Galaxy's turbulent past. My main interest is to help unravel the puzzle of the history of the Milky Way.

I am enthusiastic about projects related to teaching astronomy and scientific outreach, especially for children. It is very important for me to contribute to the dissemination of science produced within the university, as well as teaching it in an accessible way for all audiences.
