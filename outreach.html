Abrahão de Morais Observatory

The Abrahão de Morais observatory is is located in the city of Valinhos, São Paulo - Brazil, belonging to the University of São Paulo (USP). Research activities at the site were deactivated, and it is now used only to serve the local public, with sky observation activities using instruments, lectures, and workshops for children.

Atendimento Astronômico à Escolas

The Atendimento Astronômico à Escolas (literal translation: Astronomical Support for Schools) is a program of the Instituto de Astronomia, Geofísica e Ciências Atmosféricas (IAG) of the USP is a program developed to welcome middle and high schools in person and virtually. Students have contact with undergraduate and postgraduate students in astronomy and can talk about the subject.

Astronomia ao Meio-Dia

The Astronomia ao Meio-Dia (literal translation: Astronomy at Noon) is a seminar project from the Astronomy department at USP aimed at beginning undergraduate students, addressing research topics in astronomy. I work on the coordination team. All lectures are in Portuguese and are on YouTube. You can check it out using the button below

ASTROMINAS

 "Astrominas is a project developed and executed only by women, with the aim of facilitating young students' access to university, and strengthening their contact with women scientists. The “fairy godmothers” accompany a group of students for 3 weeks, helping to develop some activities."

Cecilia Project

"The Cecilia Project is a program developed by IAG/USP that brings scientific outreach activities in the area of ​​earth and universe sciences to public schools. We bring knowledge such as astronomical observation techniques and telescopes, activities to recognize meteorological and geophysical phenomena, and a broader discussion about the evolution of life, our planet, and the Universe."
